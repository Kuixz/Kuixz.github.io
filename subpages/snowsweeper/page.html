Snowsweeper Nov 2022-Present
Project

Snowsweeper is a Godot-based variant of Minesweeper with elements inspired by factory games. It tends to go on and off the backburner a lot, and is still in an incomplete (alpha) stage.

I had intended to make a small Minesweeper app to put on my personal statement as a small achievement. But then the idea to add in factory elements came along. So for four or five consecutive term breaks I have promised myself that I would finish Snowsweeper before the next term period.

Yeah, no.

Well, at least progress has been made. The idea has been with me in periods of low progress and low motivation, through the ups and downs. In fact, I switched everything to Godot around January of 2024 in response to Unity's pricing changes. The perks of indie, I guess.

Efforts have been... not consistent, but at least recurrent. By the time I graduate I will definitely have finished Snowsweeper. Slowly but surely. I promise. I hope.
