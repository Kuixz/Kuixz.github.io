PPUC 2022-Present

The Hub Sheet is my one-stop shop for information and calculation concerning a mobile tower defense game I highly recommend: Arknights, a Chinese tactical tower defense that many people (me included) praise for its rich story and great music.

The Hub Sheet has informed my resource acquisition and distribution strategy since its conception in 2022 and has been continuously refined ever since. The star of the show, the Precision Pulls Until Calculator sheet, was inspired by u/lhc987’s Pulls Until Calculator and mainly serves to add aesthetic and precision without compromising existing functionality.

Quite soon, I intend to leverage the web scraping experience I gained from DeltaG to add sheets that automatically grab data taken from other sites and make my Hub Sheet an even better resource for players old and new.
